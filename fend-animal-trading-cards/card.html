Cheetah

The cheetah is the fastest land animal in the world!

  • Movement: The cheetah steers with their tail when they are running.
  • Sounds: Cheetahs do not roar like lions. Instead, they purr or growling.
  • Grouping: Males stay in groups of 2-3 while females are solitary.
  • Habitat: Cheetahs can be found in grasslands, savannnahs and mountain terrain.

With their light body weight and blunt claws, cheetahs are not well designed to protect themselves or their prey. When a larger or more aggressive animal approaches a cheetah in the wild, it will give up its catch to avoid a fight. A cheetah has amazing eyesight during the day and can spot prey from 5km away.
